## Tracking & Attribution Strategy

### Set Up Tracking Systems
1. **Pretty Links Pro** or **ThirstyAffiliates** for link cloaking and tracking
2. **Google Tag Manager** for conversion tracking
3. **Spreadsheet tracking** for:
   - Which affiliate programs are joined
   - Commission structures
   - Login credentials
   - Performance metrics

### UTM Parameter Strategy
Use consistent UTM parameters for all affiliate links:
```
?utm_source=yourblog&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=week1-foundation
```

## Legal Compliance

### Required Disclosures
1. Add affiliate disclosure to every blog post with affiliate links
2. Create a dedicated Affiliate Disclosure page
3. Use clear language: "This post contains affiliate links. If you purchase through these links, I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you."

### Example Disclosure Text:
```
This article contains affiliate links to products and services I recommend.
If you purchase through these links, I earn a commission at no additional
cost to you. I only recommend tools I've personally researched or used.
Your support helps me continue creating valuable content.
```
